HOW TO READ THIS CHART

  • Numbers show the distance in semitones from the root (0).
  • Example: In E7 = E (0), G# (4), B (7), D (10).
  • Play the notes in any order on the guitar.

KEY IDEAS

Building blocks
  • Major 3rd = 4
  • Minor 3rd = 3
  • Perfect 5th = 7
  • Minor 7th = 10
  • Major 7th = 11

QUICK RULES (MEMORY SHORTCUTS)

  • m (minor) → lower the 3rd (4 → 3)
  • 7 (dominant) → add b7 (10)
  • maj7 → add 7 (11)
  • 9 → add 9 (14)
  • sus2 / sus4 → replace the 3rd with 2 or 4
  • dim → lower the 5th (7 → 6)
  • aug → raise the 5th (7 → 8)
